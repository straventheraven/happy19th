I must’ve passed out at some point, but I woke up feeling completely fine again. Officially freaked out, I contacted everyone I had seen in the past 14 days to let them know about my symptoms and made my way to an emergency care where I was given a variety of tests. After a grueling wait, the doctor came back and told me I tested positive for strep throat. Who would’ve thought finding out you had strep throat could feel like such a blessing. I went home with my prescription of penicillin relieved and grateful. That night however things got worse.
