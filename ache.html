Then the fever came. With a temperature of 101.3 my body felt so cold I thought I was going to freeze to death. I cranked the heater to the highest setting and covered myself with 4 blankets while wearing a hoodie, sweatpants and a robe. Desperate to feel warmth from a body that no longer felt like my own, I spent the night curled up in a tight ball shivering in a pool of my own sweat.
